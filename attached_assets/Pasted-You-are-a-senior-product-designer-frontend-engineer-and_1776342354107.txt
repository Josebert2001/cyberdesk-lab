MAIN GOAL
Transform the current interface into a more polished, engaging, responsive, accessible, and intuitive experience while preserving the core product structure and cybersecurity identity.

- layout inconsistencies
- spacing problems
- weak visual hierarchy
- repetitive UI patterns
